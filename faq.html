Frequently Asked Questions

Below are answers to common questions about how we work, what we do and how to start a cooperation.

What exactly is your role in an energy project?

We act as an independent Energy Solutions Coordinator and Project Connector.

Our role is to structure the project, align technical solutions, source suitable suppliers and coordinate communication between all involved parties.

We do not sell equipment directly.

Do you work with specific brands or manufacturers?

No. We operate independently and are not tied to specific brands or manufacturers.

This allows us to select solutions based on project requirements rather than product availability.

How can we start working together?

The process usually starts with a short discussion of your requirements.

You can contact us via the website form or directly by email or messenger. After an initial review, we define the next steps and scope of coordination.

What information do you need from us?

At the initial stage, basic information is sufficient:

  • project location
  • power or energy requirements
  • application type (backup, prime, hybrid, etc.)
  • expected timeline

Additional details can be clarified step by step.

In which regions do you operate?

We currently coordinate projects across Europe, Ukraine and Africa.

Cross-regional and international projects are also possible.

Do you provide installation or EPC services?

We do not perform installation ourselves.

When required, we coordinate and align suitable EPC contractors or local partners as part of the project.

How are your services structured commercially?

Our services are typically structured as coordination, sourcing or consulting engagements.

The commercial structure depends on project scope and is defined clearly before starting.

Can you support early-stage feasibility?

Yes. We often support projects at an early stage by clarifying requirements, comparing solution options and identifying potential risks.
