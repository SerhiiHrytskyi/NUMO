How We Work

Energy projects require clear structure, coordination and accountability. Our 5-step process ensures alignment from start to finish.

1

Requirements Analysis

We start by understanding the technical, operational and regional requirements of the project.

This includes:

  • power demand and usage profile
  • site conditions and environment
  • grid availability and reliability
  • project timeline and priorities

The goal is to avoid assumptions and define clear parameters.

2

Solution Design

Based on the requirements, we define suitable solution concepts.

This may include:

  • generator-based systems
  • solar PV solutions
  • energy storage options
  • hybrid system combinations

We focus on practical and reliable configurations, not theoretical models.

3

Supplier Sourcing & Verification

We identify and evaluate suitable manufacturers, distributors and partners.

Our sourcing process includes:

  • supplier capability assessment
  • technical specification alignment
  • availability and delivery feasibility
  • basic compliance and documentation review
4

Coordination & Alignment

We coordinate communication between all involved parties.

This includes:

  • technical clarifications
  • commercial alignment
  • logistics and delivery planning
  • documentation flow

Our role is to reduce friction and ensure clarity.

5

Project Support & Handover

We support the project through delivery and handover phases.

This may include:

  • coordination during delivery
  • documentation support
  • alignment with local partners
  • transition to operation or EPC teams

What This Process Delivers

  • Reduced technical and procurement risks
  • Clearer decision-making
  • Better supplier fit
  • Fewer delays and misunderstandings
  • Transparent and documented coordination

Working Formats

Depending on project scope and requirements, we can work in different formats:

  • Full project coordination (from requirements to delivery)
  • Supplier sourcing and verification only
  • Technical consulting and feasibility support
  • One-time advisory or second opinion

The format is defined together based on your specific needs.

Frequently Asked Questions

Do you sell equipment directly?

No. We do not sell generators, solar panels or batteries directly. Our role is to coordinate energy solutions ‚Äî which includes sourcing, supplier verification, technical alignment and project coordination.

How do you select suppliers?

We evaluate suppliers based on technical capability, compliance documentation, delivery track record and suitability for specific project requirements. We maintain independence and do not favor any single manufacturer.

What regions do you operate in?

We primarily work across Europe (EU compliance markets), Ukraine (reconstruction & resilience projects) and Africa (off-grid & industrial power). We adapt solutions to regional standards and logistics.

How long does the coordination process take?

It depends on project complexity. Simple requirements analysis and supplier sourcing may take 1-2 weeks. Full project coordination from requirements to delivery support can range from weeks to months.

Do you provide installation or EPC services?

We do not provide direct installation or EPC services. However, we can coordinate with local EPC contractors, align technical specifications and support the handover process.
