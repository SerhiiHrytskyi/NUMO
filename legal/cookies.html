Cookie Policy

Last Updated: January 9, 2026

1. What Are Cookies?

Cookies are small text files that are stored on your device (computer, tablet, or mobile) when you visit a website. They help the website recognize your device and remember information about your visit.

2. How We Use Cookies

We use cookies to:

  • Ensure the website functions properly (necessary cookies)
  • Remember your language preferences
  • Analyze how visitors use our website (analytics cookies, only with consent)
  • Improve user experience

3. Types of Cookies We Use

Strictly Necessary Cookies

These cookies are essential for the website to function and cannot be disabled. They are usually set in response to your actions, such as language selection.

Cookie Name Purpose Expiry
language Stores user's language preference Persistent
numo_cookie_consent Records cookie consent status 365 days
selectedLanguage Stores selected language for SEO Persistent

Analytics Cookies (Optional)

These cookies help us understand how visitors interact with our website. We only set these cookies with your explicit consent.

Cookie Name Purpose Expiry
_ga Google Analytics - distinguishes users 2 years
_gid Google Analytics - distinguishes users 24 hours
_gat Google Analytics - throttle request rate 1 minute

4. Legal Basis

We use cookies based on:

  • Art. 6(1)(f) GDPR - Legitimate interest for strictly necessary cookies
  • Art. 6(1)(a) GDPR - Your consent for analytics cookies
  • §25 TTDSG - Compliance with German Telecommunications Telemedia Data Protection Act

5. Managing Your Cookie Preferences

You can manage your cookie preferences at any time:

  • Click the "Cookie Settings" button below
  • Configure your browser to block or delete cookies
  • Use browser privacy/incognito mode

6. Third-Party Cookies

We may use third-party services that set cookies on your device:

  • Google Analytics: Website analytics (only with consent)
  • These third parties have their own privacy policies

7. Do Not Track (DNT)

Our website respects Do Not Track (DNT) browser settings. When DNT is enabled, we will not load analytics cookies.

8. Updates to This Policy

We may update this Cookie Policy from time to time. Changes will be posted on this page with an updated revision date.
