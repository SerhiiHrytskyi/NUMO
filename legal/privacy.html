Privacy Policy

Last Updated: January 8, 2026

1. Introduction

NUMO Energy Solutions ("we", "us", "our") respects your privacy and is committed to protecting your personal data. This privacy policy explains how we collect, use, and protect your information in compliance with the General Data Protection Regulation (GDPR).

2. Data We Collect

We collect the following types of personal data:

  • Contact Information: Name, email address, phone number, company name
  • Project Information: Details you provide about your energy projects or coordination needs
  • Technical Data: IP address, browser type, device information
  • Cookies: Session cookies and analytics data (see Cookie Policy)

3. Purpose of Data Processing

We process your personal data for the following purposes:

  • Responding to your inquiries and contact requests
  • Project coordination and solution development
  • Communication regarding our services
  • Website improvement and analytics
  • Legal compliance and contract fulfillment

4. Legal Basis for Processing

We process your data based on:

  • Your explicit consent (GDPR Art. 6(1)(a))
  • Performance of a contract (GDPR Art. 6(1)(b))
  • Legitimate interests (GDPR Art. 6(1)(f))
  • Legal obligations (GDPR Art. 6(1)(c))

5. Data Retention

We retain your personal data only as long as necessary for the purposes outlined above:

  • Contact form data: 2 years from last contact
  • Project data: Duration of project + 3 years for legal purposes
  • Analytics data: 14 months (anonymized after 6 months)

6. Data Sharing

We do not sell your personal data. We may share data with trusted partners only when necessary:

  • Project partners (with your consent)
  • Service providers (hosting, email, analytics)
  • Legal authorities (when required by law)

7. Your Rights under GDPR

You have the following rights regarding your personal data:

  • Right to Access: Request a copy of your data
  • Right to Rectification: Correct inaccurate data
  • Right to Erasure: Request deletion of your data
  • Right to Restriction: Limit processing of your data
  • Right to Data Portability: Receive your data in a structured format
  • Right to Object: Object to processing based on legitimate interests
  • Right to Withdraw Consent: Withdraw consent at any time

8. Data Security

We implement appropriate technical and organizational measures to protect your personal data against unauthorized access, loss, or destruction.

9. International Data Transfers

If we transfer data outside the EU/EEA, we ensure appropriate safeguards are in place (e.g., Standard Contractual Clauses).

10. Cookies

We use cookies to improve website functionality and analytics. You can control cookie preferences through your browser settings or our cookie banner.

11. Contact for Privacy Requests

For any privacy-related questions or to exercise your rights, please contact:

Email: privacy@yourdomain.com

Address: [Company Address]

12. Supervisory Authority

You have the right to lodge a complaint with your local data protection authority if you believe your data is not being processed in accordance with GDPR.

13. Changes to This Policy

We may update this privacy policy periodically. The "Last Updated" date at the top indicates the most recent revision.
